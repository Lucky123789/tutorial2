The Restaurant

The Black Goose Bistro is a vibrant and welcoming spot that offers a unique dining experience. Our casual lunch and dinner fare is served in a hip and trendy atmosphere, perfect for both relaxed meals and special occasions.

Signature Dishes

Our signature dishes include:

  1. The Black Goose Burger: A gourmet burger topped with caramelized onions and blue cheese.
  2. Seared Scallops: Served with a lemon butter sauce and seasonal vegetables.
  3. Truffle Mac and Cheese: Creamy mac and cheese infused with truffle oil and topped with a crispy breadcrumb crust.
  4. Grilled Salmon: Fresh salmon fillet grilled to perfection and served with a dill yogurt sauce.
  5. Chocolate Lava Cake: A rich and decadent dessert with a molten chocolate center, served with vanilla ice cream.
